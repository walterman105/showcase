Showcase

A pure-software wireless CarPlay receiver for jailbroken cellular iPads.

Compatibility

  • Rootful build for iOS 12-14, packaged as iphoneos-arm.
  • Rootless build for iOS 15-17 on Dopamine and palera1n rootless, packaged as iphoneos-arm64.
  • Requires Personal Hotspot support through an active SIM or carrier provisioning.

Notes

Showcase takes over classic Bluetooth while active and restores Apple's bluetoothd when stopped. Diagnostics and packet dumps are off by default. Enable them from the info button only when collecting a report.
